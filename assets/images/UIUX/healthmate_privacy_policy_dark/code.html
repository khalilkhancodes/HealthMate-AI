Privacy Policy

At HealthMate, your health data is your most private asset. This policy outlines how we protect, encrypt, and handle your biological information with clinical-grade security.

analytics

1. Information We Collect

We collect biometric and health-related data to provide precise insights into your physical performance. This includes:

  • check_circle Vital Statistics: Heart rate variability, blood oxygen levels, and sleep cycles retrieved from connected wearables.
  • check_circle Activity Logs: Geographic coordinates during workouts (only when enabled), step counts, and caloric expenditure.
  • check_circle User Input: Dietary logs, mood tracking, and health goals you manually enter into the Obsidian dashboard.
verified_user

2. Clinical-Grade Security

Your data is stored using AES-256 end-to-end encryption. HealthMate employs decentralized storage protocols to ensure that even in the event of a system-wide breach, individual identities remain anonymized and decoupled from biological markers.

Zero-Knowledge

We cannot read your personal health records; only your device holds the decryption keys.

Biometric Auth

Access to sensitive analytics requires mandatory FaceID or fingerprint verification.

share_off

3. Third-Party Sharing

HealthMate does not sell your personal data to insurance companies, data brokers, or advertisers. We only share data in the following strictly defined scenarios:

Research Partnerships: Aggregated, fully de-identified data may be shared with medical institutions for large-scale epidemiological studies, only if you opt-in.

Emergency Services: Vital data can be shared with emergency responders if the "Crisis Protocol" feature detects a cardiac event or severe fall.
